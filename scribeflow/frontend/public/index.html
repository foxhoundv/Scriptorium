Characters

Quickly add character pages to your project's Binder. Each name you enter will become a dedicated document in the Characters folder, ready for notes, backstory, and descriptions. Leave a field blank to skip it.

Places

Quickly add location pages to your project's Binder. Each name you enter will become a dedicated document in the Places folder, ready for descriptions, maps, and lore. Leave a field blank to skip it.

Hot-Links

Link character, place, and item pages so you can reference them instantly while writing.

Enable Hot-Links
When enabled, typing hl/ anywhere in the editor opens a quick-search menu showing your tagged pages. Select one to insert a clickable badge inline — click the badge while writing to instantly view that character's or place's synopsis and notes without leaving the page.
💡 Type hl/ followed by a name while writing to search — up to 10 matches appear. Use arrow keys to navigate, Enter to insert.
